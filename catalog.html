Why Choose Raff Coffee Original?

All of our products come from selected coffee beans obtained directly from local farmers in West Lampung, Lampung Province, Indonesia. With a maintained process from picking to packaging, we ensure that each product has the best quality and authentic taste of Lampung coffee.

Roasted Coffee Beans

  • Suitable for those of you who want to grind your own coffee to get a fresh aroma and taste every time you brew it.
  • The roasting process is carried out using a special technique to produce a distinctive flavor character.

Milled Coffee

  • Practical for those of you who want ease in brewing coffee.
  • Our coffee grounds are processed to the perfect texture, keeping the aroma and taste intact until they reach your hands.

Green Beans

  • An ideal choice for roasters or coffee lovers who want to experiment with their own roasting process.
  • Guaranteed fresh and straight from the farmer, giving you complete control over the final coffee result.
