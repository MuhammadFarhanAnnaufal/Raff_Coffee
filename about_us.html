ABOUT US

Welcome to Raff Coffee, a start-up coffee company founded by local youth with a passion for bringing quality coffee from the land of Lampung. We believe that every cup of coffee you enjoy has a story, starting from the selected coffee beans that we get directly from farmers in West Lampung Regency, Lampung Province, Indonesia.

our commitment

We are committed to providing the best coffee experience, from the farm to your cup. Our process involves close collaboration with local farmers, using the best processing methods, and serving the perfect coffee for coffee lovers. Every step is carried out with full dedication in order to provide more value to every product we produce.

Authentic Taste from Lampung, Indonesia

Lampung is known as one of the best coffee producing areas in Indonesia. At Raff Coffee, we want to present the authentic taste of Lampung coffee beans which are rich in taste and distinctive aroma. By establishing direct relationships with farmers, we ensure that the coffee beans we choose are carefully processed to maintain the best quality.

Introducing Lampung Coffee to the World

Raff Coffee has a big vision: to bring Lampung coffee to the world stage. We believe that the marketing potential of local Indonesian coffee, especially Lampung, must be maximized so that it is known to coffee lovers in various parts of the world.

Local Potential, Global Proud

By using genuine coffee beans from West Lampung, we support the local economy while introducing the uniqueness of Indonesian coffee to the world. We believe that working closely with local farmers is an important first step to realizing this vision.

Experience the Story in Every Taste

For us, coffee is not just a drink; it is culture, tradition, and stories. Together with Raff Coffee , let's taste the authentic taste and support local coffee's journey to become the pride of the world.

“We are bringing Lampung coffee to the world stage”
